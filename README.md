# Sassy - Business Chatbot

## What is Sassy?
Sassy is an AI-powered chatbot built to answer customer questions about my business. She's deployed and live on my Instagram bio.

## The Problem it Solves
Customers visiting my Instagram profile often have questions about my business. Instead of waiting for a manual reply, Sassy answers them instantly and accurately.

## How I Built It
- Designed the concept and vision myself
- Created a sketch of the logo and used Kimi AI to generate the final logo
- Built and prompted using Claude (Anthropic)
- Iterated on prompts until Sassy's personality and responses matched my vision

## Tools Used
- Claude by Anthropic (building + prompt engineering)
- Kimi AI (logo generation)

## Status
🟢 Live and deployed on Instagram
